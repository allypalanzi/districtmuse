about

Muse is a proof-of-concept web app that makes every commute a creative experience.

check in

Muse can be used in or around any metro station. Check in whenever you are close by!

create art

Use your toolbox and a piece of the DC metro map to create your own representation of the weekly theme!

favorite images

In the gallery, you can view submissions from other metro goers and favorite the best images.

digital canvas

Digital Canvases bring Muse to life by showcasing works of art in metro stations around the city. Submit your drawings for a chance to see your own creation displayed!

process

A step-by-step look at the making of Muse.

Research

We first started by visiting metro stations and bus stops around the city and conducting a survey.

Storyboards

Then we identified that commuters need a creative way to pass the time at the metro; we showed this problem/solution scenario in our storyboard.

Logo Concepts

We brainstormed names and developed logos for the app.

Wireframing

Then we mocked up simple wireframes of what elements and pages the app would include.

Paper Prototype

We built a paper prototype from our wireframes and held user testing sessions.

Style Tiles

We developed moodboards and style tiles that helped us with the initial planning phase of the design.

Initial Design

We designed our app through collaboration and teamwork!
